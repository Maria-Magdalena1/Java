# Java Advanced

## Fill the Matrix

### Description
    Filling a matrix regularly (top to bottom and left to right) is boring. Write two methods that fill
    a size N x N matrix in two different patterns. Both patterns are described below:

    1 5 9 13      1 8 9 16
    2 6 10 14     2 7 10 15
    3 7 11 15     3 6 11 14
    4 8 12 16     4 5 12 13
